About Me

I'm Maria Christina Reta.

A fresh graduate with a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology from the Polytechnic University of the Philippines – Sto. Tomas. I’m passionate about using my skills in design and technology to glorify God and lead others closer to Jesus.

As a dedicated member of the multimedia team at Lift Jesus Cell Church, I create graphics, branding kits, and presentations, and help manage our social media presence. I’m also working part-time as a web designer, where I focus on building purposeful and user-friendly websites. I believe my creativity is a gift from God—and I aim to use it faithfully, knowing that without Him, I am nothing.
